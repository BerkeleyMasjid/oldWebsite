Youth and Young Adult Activities

 

Youth Group


In February 2015 Berkeley Masjid student liaisons - in conjunction with Berkeley High MSA - established a weekly youth group for high school brothers and sisters. During these youth groups, we address issues that Muslim youth face in our community, create mentor / mentee relationships between college and high school Muslim students, and establish the mosque as a space for the youth.


Youth Groups are held on Fridays after Asr prayer at Berkeley Masjid. If you/your child would like to join our youth group, feel free to contact the Berkeley Masjid Student Liaisons, Aafreen Mahmood and Sammy Mehtar.


Boy Scouts


In April 2015, Berkeley Masjid began a Boy Scouts troop for young boys who are between the ages 7 and 18. If you are interested in learning more about the Boy Scouts troop, please contact Sister Kristin George.

Sunday School


The Berkeley Masjid Sunday School serves families with children in preschool through 12th grade. Children learn to read Quran and study the fundamentals of Islamic creed, and prayer and purification.  They also learn about the life of the Prophet (PBUH) and his companions (RA).


Beginning in May of 2015, we began enrolling new families on a first-come, first-served basis. Enrollment closed on June 10th.  Not later than July 10, 2015, we intend to notify families of our admission decisions.   Admitted families will have until August 1st to submit payment of $100 per child in order to secure their enrollment.
