Activities at Berkeley Masjid

Berkeley Masjid activities range from ad hoc community barbecues, to volunteer events, to hosting legal clinics serve to educate our community on civil rights protections.  That said, the bulk of our activities centers around children and young adults. These activities are regular;  they include Sunday School and Boy Scouts.  Read More.

Quranic and Arabic Language Studies Classes.  Currently, Brother Mus’ab Abdallah is teaching Tahfeez ul Qur’an classes on Saturday evenings at Berkeley Masjid. If you would like to learn more about this Qur’an memorization class, please contact Brother Mus’ab.

Ramadan Events & Volunteer Opportunities

During the month of Ramadan, Berkeley Masjid includes Iftar meals and Taraweeh prayers in its daily activities. 

In addition to arranging community iftars, during our most sacred month, our volunteers help plan community events, classes, and activities for the youth. If you would like to get involved in volunteering at Berkeley Masjid during Ramadan 2015, please fill out this volunteer form.
